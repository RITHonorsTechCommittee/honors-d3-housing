Administrative Tasks

Manage Rooms
Rooms numbers and occupancy levels are shown under Manage Rooms. Remove rooms by clicking the "x" next to the room number. Add rooms (or replace the whole list) by clicking "Manage Rooms" and then selecting the appropriate button. If there is a room with the wrong occupancy, contact Tech Committee
Manage Students
The student list shows the email addresses that are allowed to register for rooms. Manage them the same way you would manage rooms.
Manage Admins
Administrators have full access to the housing website including changing lists and downloading the list of reservations.
Manage Editors
Editors are students in Tech Committee who have limited access the the housing website, for correcting changes. Unless you are in tech committee, you probably don't need to worry about this box.
